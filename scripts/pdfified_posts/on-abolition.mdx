# On Abolition

## 2022-12-05

### "Abolition reading list."

---

First, prerequisite thoughts in [On Black Liberation](/posts/on-black-liberation) and prior articles. Reading those might be helpful prerequisites to the following books about abolition. This page is (so far) only going to contain links to others' work on abolition, in case you'd like to learn more about it. I haven't yet gotten to all these, so this list is also for my sake.

#### If you're new to abolition:
- [We Do This 'Til We Free Us: Abolitionist Organizing and Transforming Justice](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/56019003-we-do-this-til-we-free-us) by Mariame Kaba

#### If you need something concise & easy:
- ["Prisons Make Us Safer": And 20 Other Myths about Mass Incarceration](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/54615850-prisons-make-us-safer) by Victoria Law
- [Are Prisons Obsolete?](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/108428.Are_Prisons_Obsolete_) by Angela Y. Davis

#### If you're an academia nerd:
- [Abolition Geography: Essays Towards Liberation](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/55566309-abolition-geography) by Ruth Wilson Gilmore

#### If you wanna feel hopeful for the future:
- [Freedom Dreams: The Black Radical Imagination](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/90528.Freedom_Dreams) by Robin D.G. Kelley

#### If you wanna understand how we got here:
- [America on Fire: The Untold History of Police Violence and Black Rebellion Since the 1960s](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/55298326-america-on-fire) by Elizabeth Hinton
- [Assata: An Autobiography](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/100322.Assata) by Assata Shakur
- [Blood in the Water: The Attica Prison Uprising of 1971 and Its Legacy](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/28007898-blood-in-the-water) by Heather Ann Thompson

#### Want to hear from a lot of different voices:
- [Abolition For The People](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/58021042-abolition-for-the-people) by Colin Kaepernick

#### Don't understand what comes after we get rid of the cops?
- [Beyond Survival: Strategies and Stories from the Transformative Justice Movement](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/51778952-beyond-survival) edited by Leah Lakshmi Piepzna-Samarasinha and Ejeris Dixon
- [Mutual Aid: Building Solidarity in This Crisis (And the Next)](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/54654726-mutual-aid) by Dean Spade
- [The Care We Dream of: Liberatory and Transformative Approaches to LGBTQ+ Health](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/58991274-the-care-we-dream-of) edited by Zena Sharman
